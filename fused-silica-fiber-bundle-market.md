<p><p>The fused silica fiber bundle market is categorized into two main types: single branch fiber bundles and multi-branch fiber bundles. Single branch fiber bundles consist of a single set of fibers, ideal for applications requiring minimal light loss and direct transmission, often used in fiber optics communications and sensors. In contrast, multi-branch fiber bundles contain multiple branches, allowing for light to be distributed across various paths. This versatility makes them suitable for complex imaging systems and medical applications, enhancing functionality and performance.</p></p>
