<p><p>The phosphorus-containing epoxy resin market can be categorized into two main types based on their formulation methods: chemical reaction and physical addition. </p><p>Chemical reaction types involve the covalent bonding of phosphorus within the epoxy matrix, enhancing flame retardant properties and thermal stability through a molecular transformation. </p><p>In contrast, physical addition types involve dispersing phosphorus compounds into the epoxy resin without altering the chemical structure, improving fire resistance and low smoke emission characteristics, making them suitable for various applications in coatings and composites.</p></p>
