<p><strong>The Electrophotographic Printing in Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aqueous</li><li>Latex</li><li>Solvent</li><li>UV-Cured</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrophotographic printing in the packaging market primarily involves four key technologies: aqueous, latex, solvent, and UV-cured inks. Aqueous inks are water-based, offering quick drying and vibrant colors, ideal for non-porous substrates. Latex inks, also water-based, provide excellent adhesion and durability. Solvent inks contain organic solvents, delivering high durability for outdoor use but with more volatile emissions. UV-cured inks use ultraviolet light for immediate curing, enabling high-speed production with strong adhesion; they are suitable for a variety of substrates. Each type caters to specific packaging needs.</p></p>
