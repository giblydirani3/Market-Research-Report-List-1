<p><ul><li>Two Way Plug Valve</li><li>Three Way Plug Valve</li><li>Four Way Plug Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual plug valve market consists of three main types: two-way, three-way, and four-way plug valves. Two-way plug valves control the flow of fluid in a single direction, typically used for on/off service. Three-way plug valves facilitate flow diversion among three ports, allowing for mixing or splitting of fluids. Four-way plug valves offer even more versatility, managing flow across four ports, which can be used for complex routing in larger systems. Each type serves distinct applications in various industries.</p></p>
