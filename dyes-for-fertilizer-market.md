<p><p>The Dyes for Fertilizer market includes two primary types: Powder and Liquid. Powder dyes are dry, granular substances that are mixed with fertilizers to provide color, aiding in product identification and uniformity. They are easy to store and handle, with long shelf lives. Liquid dyes are pre-dissolved solutions that offer quick and even dispersion when blended with fertilizers. They are ideal for precise color application and are often preferred for their convenience and consistency in large-scale operations. Both types enhance fertilizer aesthetics and functionality.</p></p>
