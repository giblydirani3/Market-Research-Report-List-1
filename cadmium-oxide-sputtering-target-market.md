<p><p>The cadmium oxide sputtering target market comprises two main types: rotary type and non-rotatable type. Rotary type targets are designed to rotate during the sputtering process, enhancing material deposition uniformity and efficiency, making them ideal for large-area applications. Non-rotatable targets, in contrast, are fixed in place and are typically used for smaller or more specialized applications where uniformity is less critical. Both types cater to various industries, including electronics, optics, and thin-film coatings, depending on specific requirements.</p></p>
