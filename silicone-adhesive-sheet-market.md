<p><p>The silicone adhesive sheet market is categorized into single-sided and double-sided types. Single-sided adhesive sheets feature adhesive on one side, providing a simple solution for various applications, such as bonding or sealing materials without the need for a complex installation process. In contrast, double-sided adhesive sheets have adhesive on both sides, allowing for more versatile applications, such as mounting and laminating materials. Both types are widely used in industries like automotive, electronics, and construction for their excellent adhesion and temperature resistance.</p></p>
