<p><p>The Ultra Pure Dimethyl Carbonate market is primarily categorized based on production methods: the Propylene Oxide Method and the Ethylene Oxide Method. The Propylene Oxide Method involves the reaction of dimethyl carbonate with propylene oxide, offering a high purity product suitable for pharmaceuticals and high-performance applications. In contrast, the Ethylene Oxide Method uses ethylene oxide as a reactant, resulting in a different purity profile and suitability for various industrial applications. Each method has distinct efficiency, cost, and purity implications.</p></p>
